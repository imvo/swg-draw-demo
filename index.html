How to use:

Place three points on the area by clicking.

You will receive parallelogram and circle, plotted on points' base.

You can find info about parallelogram's and circle's area "S", which is the same for them, under the figures.

Also there you can find info about red points' coordinates

Feel free to gently move red points around - when you finish figures and coordinates data will be updated.

Use "Reset" button to start over.

All values are shown in pixels.
